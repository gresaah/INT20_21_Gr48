WHO CANNOT DONATE BLOOD?

Among the temporary conditions are:



Pregnancy

Acute fever

Recent alcoholic intake

Ear or body piercing and tattooing

Surgery
Persons are not allowed to donate blood anyime:

Cancer
Cardiac disease
Sever lung disease
Hepatitis B and C

Other conditions or disease stated in the Guide to Medical Assessment of Blood Donors.
