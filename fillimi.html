Biomedical Service

Blood Group Serology

With decades of experience in transfusion medicine and serology, the Red Cross offers the expertise and technology to help you achieve the best possible outcomes for your patients. The NRLBGS assists with the identification of red blood cell (RBC) antibodies, RBC phenotyping and crossmatching, and provides matched RBC units for patients requiring phenotype-selected RBC products in the management of sickle cell disease, as well as antigen-negative and IgA-deficient blood products.

Service

Leukopaks

Collected via leukapheresis in FDA-registered collection centers from IRB-consented, pre-screened healthy donors, the Red Cross can confidently and repeatedly supply you with quality starting materials that drives speed and efficiency in your cell sourcing processes.

Autologous Mononuclear Cell Collection Services

Economies of scale and cross-utilization of technology allow us to offer you cost-effective autologous collection services and provide high-quality source material for commercial production as well for clinical trials.

Biopharma Contract Manufacturing

EOur team of experts can provide you with high-quality material, hands-on support, national collections, and testing services—ensuring consistency with your manufacturing processes and providing manufacturing capacity that can scale according to your needs.

Buffy Coats

Generic buffy coats are manufactured from whole blood collected from a general blood donor. Corresponding donor demographic information on generic buffy coats is not available. Also, specific donor selection and donor recall ability for subsequent Leukopak product donations are not possible.
